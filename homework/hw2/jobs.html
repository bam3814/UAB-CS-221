Work at JavaJam

Positions:

Janitor

A janitor's main job is to maintain JavaJam's reputation of cleanliness. They would need to be willing to have night shifts if need be. They need to sweep, mop, and take out garbage. They need to wipe down and sanitize tables.

  • Sweep
  • Clean tables
  • Take trash out
  • $13 per hour

Barista

A barista needs to be able to be cordial and sincere to all of their customers. They would be responsible for cleaning all the equipment, restocking supplies, and maintaining a clean environment. They would need to work on the weekends and work on very busy days. They need to know enough about the menu to suggest items to customers.

  • Prepare drinks
  • Serve drinks
  • Free coffee during shifts
  • $15 per hour
